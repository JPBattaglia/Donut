This location was opened in 1995 and has been family owned and operated for 25 years. Starting off as a small donut shop, we have grown throughout the years and serve a various selection of donuts, Pastry’s, Bagels, Sandwiches and Breakfast dishes. Right in the center of Chinatown, Little Italy and the Financial district, come by and join us for one of our most popular donuts – The Apple Fritter – or if you have a bigger appetite, the All-American Breakfast. Come visit us in the historic San Francisco Chinatown district. We are located right across the street from Columbus Tower (The Sentinal Building), a block away from Transamerica Pyramid, a block away from Hilton Financial District, in walking distance of the Dragon’s Gate (Chinatown entrance). Union Square shopping district, North Beach Little Italy, Coit Tower and a bike ride away from Ghirardelli Square and Pier 39 in Fisherman’s Wharf.
